Overview

Our application, SRGT School Management, is designed to bridge the gap between school administration, teachers, and parents using the Salesforce platform.

Key Modules

  • Student Information System: Complete digital record of students and classes.
  • Attendance Management: Track daily presence with automated monthly reports.
  • Examination Portal: Manage marks, grades, and generate digital marksheets.
  • Teacher Dashboard: Simple interface for teachers to update daily classroom activities.

Technical Excellence

Developed under the leadership of Director Dheeraj Gour, the system utilizes REST APIs for seamless integration with other school tools.
